A few words about us
We are boutique hospitality experts. We provide luxury hospitality experiences to travellers. Our bespoke collection of boutique villas and hotels combined with unique Sri Lankan experiences ensures you get a taste of everything; from sun, sea and mountains to adventure, history and culture.

Our story

Our journey began when we recognized a gap in the visibility of luxury properties owned by individuals who have the passion to promote tourism in Sri lanka, but lacked the expertise to execute it. With the combined expertise of our team, we believed we could provide a solution to this. Thus began Zēnəth Villas; Zēnəth translates to “the time at which something is most powerful or successful” , which is what we always strive for. We aspire to enhance visibility and awareness of these properties by effectively showcasing and pairing them with specialized services to offer a comprehensive luxury experience.
